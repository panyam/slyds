---
name: slyds-slides
description: List all slides in the deck with position, layout, and title
allowed-tools: Bash(slyds *)
---

List all slides in the presentation:

1. Run: `slyds ls --json`
2. Display as a formatted table:
   - Position (1-based)
   - Filename
   - Layout type
   - Title (from h1)
3. Show total slide count
